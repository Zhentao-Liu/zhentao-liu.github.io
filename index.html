Zhentao Liu

I’m currently a Ph.D. student in the IMPACT Lab at ShanghaiTech University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhiming Cui and Prof. Dinggang Shen. Previously, I earned my bachelor’s degree from Ningbo University, China, in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Qiuping Jiang. My current research interests include 3D/4D vision & reconstruction, neural representation, and medical image reconstruction.
